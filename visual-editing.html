My Editing Process

01

Beat Mapping

I analyze the music track to identify key beats, transitions, and rhythmic patterns that will guide the visual editing.

02

Visual Collection

Gathering visual elements that match the mood and theme of the music, ensuring cohesion throughout the piece.

03

Precise Synchronization

Frame-by-frame editing to match visual transitions with musical beats, creating a seamless audio-visual experience.

04

Effect Application

Adding dynamic effects that enhance the rhythm and flow, with careful attention to intensity and timing.
