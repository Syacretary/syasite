My Editing Process

01

Search for musics

I look for music that feels meaningful to me and is also really 'editable' – you know, easy to sync edits with. I usually search for music that matches whatever vibe I'm feeling. Like, if I'm happy, I'll typically choose music that's more upbeat and energetic.

02

Create a concept

Usually, the concept just clicks for me as soon as I hear the music. I immediately start picturing, like, 'what if this song became a visual editing masterpiece?' If I'm totally stuck though, it's totally cool to look for references or inspiration

03

Start project

So, I start a new project, throw in some music, and BAM! My ideas and the whole concept just vanish into thin air. Seriously, it's like my brain does a total reset. And you know what happens next? Procrastination city, population: me. We're talking days, even months, of putting it off. Oh, and let's not forget the secret ingredient: a big ol' sprinkle of laziness.
